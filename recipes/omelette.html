This is an omelette.

Ingredients

  • Eggs x2

  • Milk x5 tbsp

  • Salt

  • Pepper

  • Oil

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into bowl

  2. Whisk eggs

  3. Add salt and pepper

  4. Whisk again to mix in salt and pepper

  5. Get a pan, add in oil and put pan on the stove on high heat.

  6. When oil is hot, add in the eggs

  7. Spread eggs evenly around the pan so the omelette cooks evenly

  8. turn stove heat to medium

  9. Wait at least 1 or 2 mins, before using a spatula to check if the bottom of the omelette has cooked
    If it has, flip the omlette on its other side so that side can cook too
    Flip it carefully so that the omelette does not break 

  10. Wait another 1 or 2 mins before plating the omelette

FINISHED!
